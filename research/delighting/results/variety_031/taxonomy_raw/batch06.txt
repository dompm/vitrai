VARIETY: smooth flat cathedral
STRUCTURE: Flat, uniform transmissive glass with no visible body mottling or surface relief — light gray/white and evenly translucent edge to edge.
TILES: #054

VARIETY: streaky/wispy color blend
STRUCTURE: Body pattern of elongated, partially-blended color streaks (brown/turquoise, red/green/white, teal/blue, red/purple/white) drifting through a translucent or semi-opaque base, giving a smoky, marbled look rather than sharp stripes.
TILES: #055, #057, #060, #061

VARIETY: granite/ripple fine relief
STRUCTURE: Fine, all-over irregular wavy-line surface relief (water-like pebbling) covering the whole tile, visible as dense short curvy strokes of light/dark contrast; sits on top of whatever body color is present.
TILES: #056, #057, #058, #059, #060, #061

VARIETY: iridized/dichroic surface sheen
STRUCTURE: A metallic rainbow/gold sheen sitting on the surface, visually distinct from and layered over the underlying body color (dark blue-black in #056, near-white/clear in #058), giving an oily or nacreous glow.
TILES: #056, #058

VARIETY: drapery fold
STRUCTURE: Large-scale rolling, arc-shaped folds suggesting draped cloth in the body/surface, distinct from the fine ripple texture in scale — broad light/dark bands curve across the tile rather than fine pebbling.
TILES: #061

VARIETY: fine stipple/speckle dot texture
STRUCTURE: Small, discrete round dot- or frost-like specks scattered evenly across a color-graduated transparent body (purple fading to cyan/pink), distinct from the linear ripple-relief seen elsewhere — texture reads as scattered dots rather than connected wavy lines.
TILES: #062
