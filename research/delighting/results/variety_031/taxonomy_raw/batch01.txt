VARIETY: dew droplet relief
STRUCTURE: Surface relief of distinct raindrop-shaped bumps scattered over a hazy, semi-cloudy translucent body; each droplet catches light as a small bright lens against darker fogged patches.
TILES: #009, #011

VARIETY: fine seed/pebble texture
STRUCTURE: Dense field of very small round dots (finer and more uniform than the dew droplets above), either fine all-over surface pebbling or embedded bubble seeds, giving a grainy sparkle across the body.
TILES: #010, #012

VARIETY: iridized surface sheen
STRUCTURE: A thin rainbow/metallic film sitting on top of the glass body, visually distinct from and layered over the underlying pebble texture; colors shift blue-purple-gold across the surface independent of any body color.
TILES: #010

VARIETY: crisp ring mottle
STRUCTURE: Body pattern of dense, overlapping circular/oval opaque or translucent blobs with fairly distinct thin-outlined ring edges, creating a bubbly, cell-like mottle.
TILES: #013, #016

VARIETY: streaky marbled mottle
STRUCTURE: Body pattern of larger, more organic and elongated blob shapes than the crisp ring mottle, with streaky/blended edges rather than clean circular outlines, giving a marbled or amoeba-like look.
TILES: #014, #017

VARIETY: streaky swirl ribbon
STRUCTURE: Elongated color streaks in the body that blend smoothly into each other in a flowing, wave-like swirl, producing ribbon-like bands of color rather than discrete blobs.
TILES: #015
