VARIETY: ring/oval mottle
STRUCTURE: Dense overlapping oval and round opaque color blobs suspended within the glass body, arranged in loose vertical streaks; a body-level pattern, not a surface texture.
TILES: #018

VARIETY: confetti shard collage
STRUCTURE: Flat, angular, non-overlapping pieces of colored glass (taupe/grey shards, or plum/teal/pink shapes) embedded in a clear/white body, resembling scattered broken confetti; a body pattern.
TILES: #020, #021

VARIETY: thread/crack streamer lines
STRUCTURE: Thin, dark or colored thread-like lines that meander or run roughly vertically through a clear/white body, resembling fine cracks or trailed threads of glass; a body pattern layered over the base.
TILES: #020, #021, #022, #023

VARIETY: seedy bubbles
STRUCTURE: Small round bubbles suspended within the glass body, visible as scattered light or dark dots; a body pattern.
TILES: #020, #021, #022

VARIETY: iridized/dichroic surface sheen
STRUCTURE: A thin rainbow/metallic sheen sitting on top of the glass body, visually distinct from and independent of the underlying color/streaks; a surface effect.
TILES: #023, #025

VARIETY: fine granite/pebbled surface relief
STRUCTURE: All-over fine, irregular pebbled surface texture giving a rippled, water-like relief across the whole tile, distinct from any body color pattern; a surface effect.
TILES: #019, #024, #025, #026
