VARIETY: smooth cathedral
STRUCTURE: Flat, uniform transmissive color in the body with no discernible surface relief or internal structure (aside from incidental dust specks); light passes evenly through.
TILES: #036

VARIETY: cloud/gradient blend
STRUCTURE: Body-level smooth diffuse transition between two colors (here orange fading into white/cream) with a soft, cloud-like irregular boundary rather than a hard edge or discrete streaks.
TILES: #037

VARIETY: granite/ripple relief
STRUCTURE: Fine, all-over irregular pebbled surface relief covering the whole tile, water-like and small in scale, sitting on top of a uniform transmissive or translucent body color.
TILES: #038, #040, #041, #042, #043

VARIETY: baroque wave overlay
STRUCTURE: Larger-scale rolling, elongated horizontal wave bands in the surface relief, layered over the finer granite texture, giving a hand-rolled/reamy look concentrated in bands rather than uniform all-over.
TILES: #039, #042

VARIETY: opalescent smooth
STRUCTURE: Dense, uniform matte body color with little to no light transmission and no visible surface or body texture, characteristic of opalescent (rather than clear cathedral) glass.
TILES: #044
